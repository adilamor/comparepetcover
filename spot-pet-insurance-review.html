Spot Pet Insurance Review (2026)

Spot is a US accident-and-illness pet insurance provider known for highly customizable plans and no upper age limits for enrollment — making it a flexible option for pets of any age.

Get a Spot Quote

What Spot covers

Spot's accident-and-illness plans cover injuries, illnesses, surgeries, diagnostics, and (notably) dental illness and behavioral conditions. You choose your annual limit, deductible, and reimbursement rate (up to 90%). Optional preventive-care add-ons cover routine items like vaccines and wellness exams.

Pros

  • No upper age limit for enrollment — good for senior pets
  • Covers dental illness and behavioral issues
  • Flexible limits, deductibles and reimbursement (up to 90%)
  • Optional wellness add-on for routine care

Cons

  • Like all insurers, pre-existing conditions are excluded
  • Pricing rises with pet age and breed
  • Routine care requires a paid add-on

Who Spot is best for

Owners who want to fine-tune coverage, and those insuring older pets that some insurers won't accept.

How Spot compares

Premiums and coverage vary by pet, breed, age and your state — see how Spot stacks up against other insurers in our full comparison, or check average costs in your state.

Does Spot cover dental?

Spot covers dental illness under its accident-and-illness plans; routine cleanings require the preventive add-on.

Is there an age limit with Spot?

No upper age limit for enrollment, which is helpful for senior pets.
